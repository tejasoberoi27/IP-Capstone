    7 Shields Protection
    All SHARP products passed the strict Japanese quality standard. SHARP TV is proven to have safe operation and high endurance under various circumstances. Now you can enjoy your viewing experience from good quality TV that is protected by 7 Shields.

    Vibration Shield
    Good carton packing design and high quality of SHARP LED TVs are proven to be vibration resistance during delivery.

    Drop Shield
    The protective performance of the packaging and the integrity of SHARP LED TVs are proven by passing the strict standard of the drop test.

    Lightning Shield
    Varistor protects the product from lightning surge through AC cable that in line with EMC standard (IEC 61000-4-2 & IEC 61000-4-5).

    Voltage Shield
    Safe product operation is ensured throughout the more severe voltage standard than product spec. (110V- 240V)

    High Temperature & Humidity Shield
    Reliable Sharp LED TVs are proven and tested based on IEC 60065 standard to adopt high temperature and humidity settings.

    Heavy Weight Shield
    The design of wall mount provision is tested with weight loading IEC 60065 standard.
    *Wall-mounted TV on the solid wall and the TV with stands have been tested and checked after conducting the heavy weight test by loading 4 times the TV set weight on the TV.

    Stability Shield
    Strong structutal stability is proven by successful stability test at the level of IEC 60065 standard.

    *SHARP products are tested according to SHARP's laboratory standards which are higher than the global safety standard (IEC Standard). All tests are carried out by trained professionals in controlled environment. Do not try this at home.
